📚 Article: Coping with Anxiety

Feeling anxious is a natural part of life, but when it becomes overwhelming, it can interfere with our daily functioning. One of the first steps in managing anxiety is learning to recognize it.

Steps to ease anxiety:

  • 🧘 Practice deep breathing (inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8)
  • 📝 Keep a worry journal to unload your thoughts
  • 🚶‍♂️ Go for a 10-minute mindful walk
  • 🎵 Listen to calming music
  • 📵 Limit screen time before bed

Remember: You're not alone. Take small steps daily — they matter 💖
