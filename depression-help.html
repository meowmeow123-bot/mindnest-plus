💛 What to Do When You're Feeling Depressed

Gentle reminders for tough times 🌦️

🛌 Rest without guilt

It’s okay to take a break. Depression is exhausting. Allow yourself to rest without pressure.

🚶‍♀️ Take a short walk

Even 5 minutes outside can help regulate your mood. Sunlight and movement are natural mood boosters.

📖 Read or watch something light

Comforting shows, favorite books, or calming podcasts can offer distraction and comfort.

🧘‍♂️ Try deep breathing

Inhale for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Do this 3 times. It slows your heart rate and calms the mind.

📱 Talk to someone

Message a friend. You don’t have to explain everything — even a meme or check-in helps reduce isolation.

🖊️ Write your feelings

Journaling can give your pain a safe space. Even one line like “Today is heavy” matters.

💧 Drink water and eat something small

Depression often makes us forget basic care. Start with a glass of water. Maybe a fruit or toast next.

🎶 Listen to gentle music

Soft instrumental or nature sounds can comfort the nervous system. Try our meditation page.

Remember: This will pass. You are doing the best you can. 💗
